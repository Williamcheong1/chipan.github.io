About us

My name is William, and I graduated from the Culinary Institute of America in Hyde Park, New York, where I majored in bakers, and my partner, Moses, graduated from the same school as me, and he majored in desserts. Since we are both alumni and classmates, we both had the idea of starting a business when we were in school. After graduation, we hit it off immediately, so we co-founded a cake company. With our exquisite cake making skills, we soon became a little famous and developed step by step to the present, a global chain of cake shops.

MC History

Our first shop was a small shop we bought in Macau in 2012, when we had just graduated, and with our superb skills, it quickly became popular on the streets and slowly made its way to the whole of Macau. As it got bigger and bigger, in 2021 we became a listed company and established our brand of cakes all over the world.
